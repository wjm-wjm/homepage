01. About Me

Hello! I am Jiangming Wang, a researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab, where I work on computer vision and machine learning. My research interests include multimodal large language models, image forensics deepfake detection, and AIGC detection. Before joining Tencent, I received my M.E. in Computer Science from East China Normal University (2024), and my B.E. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University of Technology (2021).

02. Research Interests

Multimodal LLMs

Vision–language understanding, reasoning, and alignment for next-generation foundation models.

Image Forensics

Detecting tampering, manipulation, and provenance of visual content in the wild.

Deepfake Detection

Generalizable face forgery detection across unseen manipulations and domains.

AIGC Detection

Identifying AI-generated images from diffusion models, GANs, and beyond.
